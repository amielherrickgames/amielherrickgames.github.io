How it plays

❄️

Your cannon is your engine

There's no joystick. Firing a snowball kicks your tank the other way — chain shots to skate, dodge, and line up the perfect angle.

🎯

Every enemy fights differently

Fast tanks dart, heavies shrug off hits, snipers punish you from across the lake — and reinforcements arrive in waves.

👑

Bosses, chests & daily rewards

Face a boss every 10 levels, open victory chests to upgrade your tank, and claim your Daily Frost Bonus every time you return.
